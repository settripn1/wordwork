#### Overview

JPMorganChase always speaks with clarity, authority and accessibility. Use the layouts in this template to ensure every slide aligns with brand standards.

- Use Bronze as the primary accent color
- Typography is always Black, White, or Bronze only
- Maintain generous white space — don't crowd slides
- Serif font (Tiempos Text / Georgia fallback) is for display only — headlines and titles
- Body copy always in SOMS / Calibri fallback

<div class="highlight-box">

**Key principle:** Slides should communicate one idea clearly. If a slide needs more than 4 bullet points, it should be two slides.

</div>

</div>

#### Travertine Background Slide

Use this layout when you want a warmer, softer feel — appropriate for introductory context or framing slides. The Travertine background (#F4EFE7) paired with black body text and Bronze accents maintains full brand compliance.
